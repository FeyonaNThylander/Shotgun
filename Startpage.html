Soon you'll enter the Wild West and meet your most feared opponent, a Computer in a game of *SHOTGUN*.

GAME RULES

> CLICK HERE <


YOU HAVE 4 OPTIONS IN THIS GAME:

Reload

In order to use your gun, you have to reload it with bullets.
If your opponent shoots you while you're reloading your gun, you die!

Block

When you 'Block', you're opponent looses a bullet and you're left without a scratch.

Shoot

You must have at least 1 bullet to shoot. If you both 'Shoot' at the same time, you both loose a bullet.

*Shotgun*

In order to use *Shotgun* you must have 3 bullets.
The opponent won't be able to get away even if 'Block' or 'Shoot' are used.
